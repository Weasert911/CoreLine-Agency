
## Overview
Consistent editing standards ensure high-quality video output and client satisfaction.

## Editing Process
1. **Receive Assignment** - Review project brief and requirements
2. **Initial Cut** - Create rough cut based on client specifications
3. **Internal Review** - Peer review for completeness and quality
4. **Revision Cycle** - Implement up to {{revisions}} revisions based on feedback
5. **Final Export** - Export final video in agreed format
6. **Delivery** - Upload to shared drive and mark project as Delivered

## Quality Standards
- **Resolution**: 1080p minimum, 4K when specified
- **Frame Rate**: Match source footage or as required
- **Audio Levels**: -14 LUFS target loudness
- **Color Grading**: Consistent with brand guidelines
- **File Naming**: `Client_Project_Version.mp4`

## Revision Limits
- Standard projects: up to {{revisions}} revisions
- Rush projects: up to {{rush_revisions}} revisions
- Additional revisions billed at {{additional_rate}}

## Collaboration
- Use shared project folders for asset storage
- Tag team members in comments for feedback
- Maintain version history in project notes
